The road ahead.

This is a time of change. I built my base in financial services. My friends, and even my parents, were a little surprised about that. When I told people that I had left, the reaction was overwhelmingly, "finally." My early career taught me an immense amount, though, and it helped me to turn vision into results.

It's all about connections with people we love.

Whether it's making music with friends, chatting with family, or simply cracking a beer with an old friend, the things that really make me tick are the connections I have with the people around me. I try not to let them forget it.

You can't win if you don't play.

It seems frightening to use a gambling mantra as a life mantra, but if I don't take some (calculated) risks, I will never get the chance to realize my risky dreams! The support of my friends and family makes all the difference, and helps me feel like I'm making the right move when I roll the dice.
